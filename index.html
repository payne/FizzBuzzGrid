Fizz Buzz is a common problem used in programming interviews. Printing numbers, starting at 1, but outputing Fizz for multiples of three & Buzz for multiples of five. FizzBuzz is output when the number is a multiple of both three & five. Here, colors are used:

  • Blue for Fizz (3)
  • Yellow for Buzz (5)
  • Green for FizzBuzz because blue & yellow make green
  • Shades of grey for other numbers (smaller numbers are darker)
